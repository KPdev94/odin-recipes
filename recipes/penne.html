Penne Alla Vodka

Penne Alla Vodka is a traditional Italian pasta meal, orginating in 45BC, it has been a staple for all Italian households. It was invented by the great king, Italio, for his dying wife to have one good last meal. But it wasn't. Because it cured her of her ailment and she lived forever.

    Ingredients
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 shallot, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 2 tbsp vodka
  • Salt
  • 1 lb tube pasta
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan
  • Basil for serving
    Steps
  1. In large skillet over medium heat, melt butter. Add shallot and garlic and cook until softened, 4 to 5 mins.
  2. Add tomato paste and red pepper and cook, stirring frequently until paste coats shallots and garlic and begins to darken, 5 mins.
  3. Add vodka to pot and stir to incorperate, scraping any browned bits from bottom, turn off pot.
  4. Bring large pot of salted water to a biol and cook pasta until al dente, reserve 2 cups of pasta water before draining.
  5. Return sauce to medium heat and ass 1/4 cup of pasta water and heavy cream, stirring to combine. Add half the parmesan and stir until melted. Turn off heat and stir in pasta. Fold in remaining parmesan adding more pasta water is sauce is looking dry.
  6. Season with salt if needed. Serve topped with basil and parmesan.
