Buffalo Chicken Garbage Bread

Home
    Ingredients:
  • 1 can (11oz) Pillsbury Thin Crust Pizza
  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shreaded
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1/3 cup ranch
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F, open pizza crust and press into rectangle.
  2. Spread ranch dressing on crust, add 1/4 cup of buffalo on top of ranch and spread evenly.
  3. In small bowl mix chicken with remaining sauce, place chick on top of crust evenly.
  4. Sprinkle cheddar and mozzarella all over.
  5. Start at short end and roll crust into log, place seam side down on baking sheet and bake for 15-20mins.
