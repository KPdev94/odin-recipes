Seared Ahi Tuna

Seared Ahi Tuna is a delicious meal for any king or queen of the sea. With meat wrenched from a living fossil, the tuna, it will melt in your mouth. That's from its mouthwatering goodness, not its age and fragility.

Home
    Ingredients:
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp asian toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp fresh cracked black pepper
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 2.5 lbs fresh ahi tuna, 1.5-1.75in thick
  • 3 tbsp canola or vegetable oil for searing
    Steps:
  1. In bowl, whisk toget first 7 ingredients to make marinade, pour over tuna, coating well. Cover tightly and chill several hours, turn fish once in middle of time.
  2. Heat 3 tbsp oil in large skillet - use aluminum or stainless, not nonstick or it won't sear - until oil is smoking. Sear tuna 1.5 mins per side on high heat. If tuna is less than 1.5 in thick only sear for 1 min
  3. Transfer tuna to large cutting board and let cool for 10 mins. Use sharp knife to thinly slice.
  4. Serve chilled or at room temp. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and cilantro or green onions if desired. Drizzle with srirachi mayo if desired.
