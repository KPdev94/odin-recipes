Pulled Pork Sandwhich

Home
    Ingredients:
  • 4 lb boneless pork butt
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbbp salt
  • 1 tbsp somked paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Fresh ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 12 oz lager
  • 1.5 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup dijon mustard
  • 1/3 cup apple cidar vinegar
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp worecestershire sauce
  • Toasted buns
    Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F. Trim excess pork fat, cut pork into large pieces to fit in large dutch oven.
  2. In small bowl combine brown sugar, salt, paprike, garlic powder, cumin, and onion powder. Season with black pepper. Rub all over pork.
  3. In large dutch oven over medium heat, heat oil. Working in batches cook pork until browned on all sides, 2 mins per side. Pour beer around pork and cover with lid.
  4. Bake until beginning to turn tender, about 3 hours. Uncover and continue to bake until pork is very tender and easily pulls apart with fork, about 1-2 hours.
  5. Transfer pork to cutting board, reserve pan dripping in pot and let rest until use.
  6. Into pan dripping whisk ketchup, mustard, vinegar, brown sugar, and worecestershire until combined. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Reduce to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally until slightly thickened, about 5 mins.
  7. Shred pork with 2 forks. Add meat to sauce and toss to combine. Serve warm with buns and remaining sauce alongside.
